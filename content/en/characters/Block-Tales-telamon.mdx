## The Mythology of Block Tales Telamon

Unlike traditional RPG gods who may be distant or indifferent, the lore suggests a more hands-on—if chaotic—approach. His actions are always framed as being for the ultimate benefit of the Robloxian race, even when those actions involve summoning giant snacks to crush opposing guards.
