|---|---|---|---|

| **Support** | Cleanse + buffs | **Speed** | Resource regen | Delaying cleanse too long |

If your runs fail around the same turn, your issue is likely speed tuning, not damage. Adjust order so support acts before your main DPS on key turns.

> **💡 Tip:** In block tales high scholar attempts, tune your support to act first in transition turns. Cleanse/buff before your damage unit swings.

## Phase-by-Phase Strategy and Turn Planning

Treat the boss as three practical segments. Even if internal mechanics differ by patch, this framework remains strong for 2026 clears.

### Phase 1: Stabilize and scout
Goals:
1. Confirm attack pattern rhythm.
2. Apply safe buffs/debuffs.
3. Preserve premium consumables.

Use low-cost defensive options and avoid all-in combos. If you can enter Phase 2 with high HP and full utility charges, you are ahead.

### Phase 2: Control adds and disrupt spikes
Most wipes happen here. Prioritize threats that multiply incoming damage (supports/summons) over tunneling the boss HP bar.

- Focus fire secondary targets quickly.
- Save cleanse for high-impact debuffs.
- Stagger cooldowns; don’t stack all defense in one turn.

### Phase 3: Structured burst
When the boss reaches finishing range, use a planned 2–3 turn kill sequence:

1. **Turn A:** Defensive prep + buff.
2. **Turn B:** Main burst rotation.
3. **Turn C:** Backup damage + emergency sustain.

If burst fails, reset to control mode instead of panicking into random skills.

| Phase | Win Condition | Resource Rule | Common Trap |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Phase 1** | Enter Phase 2 healthy | Spend only cheap tools | Early potion spam |
| **Phase 2** | Prevent collapse from adds/debuffs | Use cleanse with intent | Ignoring target priority |
| **Phase 3** | Execute planned finish | Commit burst in window | Forcing DPS while unstable |

This phase logic is the heart of most successful block tales high scholar clears.

## Gear, Consumables, and Prep Checklist

Preparation often decides whether block tales high scholar feels “impossible” or manageable. Build around survival first, then optimize damage once you can consistently reach final phase.

### Priority prep checklist

- Upgrade your frontliner’s core defensive piece first.
- Carry two tiers of healing: efficient sustain + emergency burst heal.
- Bring at least one cleanse item, even with a support cleanser.
- Use resistance options if your runs repeatedly fail to status pressure.
- Set hotkeys/quick access before pull so execution stays clean.

| Prep Item Type | Minimum Amount | Ideal Amount | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Basic healing** | 4 | 6–8 | Sustain chip damage |
| **Emergency healing** | 1 | 2–3 | Recover from spike turns |
| **Cleanse tools** | 1 | 2 | Remove high-impact debuffs |
| **Damage boosters** | 1 | 2 | Secure burst window |
| **Revive utility** | 0 | 1 | Recover from misplay |

> **⚠️ Warning:** If you repeatedly die with consumables still unused, that’s a timing issue. Move defensive and healing triggers earlier by one turn.

### Stat priorities (simple order)

- **Frontliner:** Defense > HP > Speed  
- **Support:** Speed > utility scaling > survivability  
- **DPS:** Attack/Crit > Speed > survivability baseline  

In many block tales high scholar runs, a “slightly slower but safer” build outperforms a glass-cannon setup over multiple attempts.

## Farming, Reclears, and Common Mistakes in 2026

Once you clear once, convert that win into a repeatable route. Reclear consistency is usually about discipline, not mechanics.

### Reclear loop

1. Start with conservative opener every run.
2. Keep the same target priority unless patch notes change behavior.
3. Log failed turns (example: “Turn 7 wipe from uncleaned debuff”).
4. Adjust one variable at a time (speed, item count, opener).

### Most common mistakes

- **Overhealing early**, then lacking resources later.
- **Ignoring speed tuning**, causing buff/debuff order problems.
- **Tunnel-vision damage**, especially during add pressure.
- **Holding cleanse too long**, turning manageable debuffs into wipes.

If your goal is faster farming after first clear, reduce risk in small steps:
- Swap one defensive substat for damage.
- Remove one emergency consumable only after 3+ clean runs.
- Keep your support speed intact; don’t sacrifice turn order for greed.

A stable block tales high scholar farming route comes from repeatable execution. Fast clears come after stability, not before.
