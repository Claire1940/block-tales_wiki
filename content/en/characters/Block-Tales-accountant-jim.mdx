## Combat Phases and Mechanics

The fight against **Block Tales accountant jim** is generally divided into three distinct phases. Understanding these transitions is the key to timing your big attacks.

### Phase 1: The Corporate Grind
In the initial phase, Jim uses standard physical attacks. He will often lunge at a player, requiring a precise "Block" timing. Missing these blocks will result in a "Dazed" status, which prevents you from using cards for one turn.

### Phase 2: The Hunger (Zombie Transition)
Once his HP drops below 60%, Jim begins his "Brains" chant. During this phase, his speed increases. He will use a grab attack that drains HP from your party and heals himself. This is the most dangerous part of the encounter for unprepared teams.

### Phase 3: The Final Audit
At 20% HP, Jim enters a frenzy. His defense drops, but his attack power skyrockets. This is the "burn phase" where you should unleash your strongest Fire-based combos to finish the fight before his area-of-effect (AoE) attacks overwhelm your healing capacity.

## Advanced Strategies for Success

While the basics will get you through the door, defeating the **Block Tales accountant jim** superboss consistently requires advanced maneuvers. 

1. **Perfect Blocking:** You must master the timing of Jim's groans. When he says "Brains," there is a 1.5-second delay before the hit box becomes active. Practice this timing in the early stages of the fight.
2. **Status Stacking:** While Jim is immune to poison, he is susceptible to "Burn." Using cards like "Fireball" or "Incendiary Trap" can deal consistent chip damage while you focus on defending.
3. **SP Management:** Do not spam high-cost cards early on. Save your SP for the second phase when you need to heal through his grab attacks.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not attempt this boss with a party level lower than 15. The stat disparity will make the "Final Audit" phase impossible to survive even with perfect blocking.
