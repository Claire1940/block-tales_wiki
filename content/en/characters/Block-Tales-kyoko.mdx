Use this sequence as a baseline, then adapt by encounter:

1. Open with utility/control skill to set pace  
2. Apply core damage skill during safe window  
3. Insert burst only when enemy is exposed  
4. Reposition and hold defensive option  
5. Re-enter with core damage as cooldowns reset

| Rotation Phase | What You Do | Common Mistake |
|---|---|---|
| **Setup** | Lead with control/utility | Opening with burst too early |
| **Pressure** | Sustain core damage | Overcommitting during danger |
| **Burst** | Commit in confirmed window | Forcing burst into shields/phases |
| **Reset** | Kite/reposition defensively | Standing still with no cooldowns |

This structure makes **Block Tales kyoko** feel much smoother, especially in longer fights where wasted casts add up quickly.

> **⚠️ Warning:** If your burst misses timing, don’t panic-cast everything else. Reset your cycle and preserve defensive options.
