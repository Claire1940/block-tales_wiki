## Detailed Attack Patterns and Counters

The **Block Tales shedletsky** boss fight is divided into distinct patterns. Recognizing the visual telegraphs for these moves is the difference between a successful run and a "Game Over" screen.

### 1. The Sword Slash (Single Target)
This is Shedletsky's basic attack. He lunges forward with his classic sword. 
*   **The Telegraph:** He raises the sword slightly behind his head before swinging.
*   **The Counter:** Press the block button exactly when the sword reaches the apex of the swing. A Perfect Block will reduce the damage to zero and potentially trigger a counter-attack if you have the right badges equipped.

### 2. The Fried Chicken Heal
Shedletsky will occasionally stop to eat a piece of Fried Chicken.
*   **The Effect:** Restores 20-50 HP.
*   **The Counter:** You cannot stop the heal once it starts, but you can out-damage it. Use this turn to apply buffs to your team or use a high-damage SP move like "Fireball" or "Power Bounce" to negate the health gain.

### 3. The Rain of Blocks (AOE)
A devastating area-of-effect attack where blocks fall from the sky.
*   **The Telegraph:** Shedletsky points upward, and shadows appear on the ground.
*   **The Counter:** This requires rhythmic blocking. Each block hits at a slightly different interval. Watch the shadows to time your presses.

> **Warning:** Failing to block the Rain of Blocks can result in a "Stun" status, leaving your character vulnerable for the next two turns.

## Step-by-Step Combat Strategy

To defeat **Block Tales shedletsky**, follow this three-phase approach designed for maximum efficiency in the 2026 meta.

### Phase 1: The Setup (100% - 70% HP)
In the opening turns, focus on setup. Do not blow all your SP immediately. Use "Softener" items or badges to reduce Shedletsky's defense. This ensures that every subsequent hit does more damage. Use your basic attacks to build up your SP meter.

### Phase 2: The Attrition (70% - 30% HP)
This is the longest part of the fight. Shedletsky will begin using his healing moves more frequently. You must maintain a steady rhythm of damage. If your HP falls below 50%, use a turn to consume a "Cola" or "Pizza" item. If you are playing in a group, ensure your roles are clearly defined: one player heals, while the others focus on the **Block Tales shedletsky** health bar.

### Phase 3: The Enraged State (Below 30% HP)
Once his health drops below 30%, Shedletsky's attack speed increases. He may attack twice in a single turn. This is the time to unleash your strongest SP moves. Use "Power Bounce" combined with an attack-boosting item like a "Hot Sauce" to finish the fight quickly before his high damage output overwhelms your healing capabilities.
