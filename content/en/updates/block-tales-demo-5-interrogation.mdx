| Choice Type | Impact on Gameplay | Recommended Scenario |

## Advanced Combat Tactics for Demo 5

The combat in Block Tales is heavily inspired by classic paper-style RPGs, where timing is everything. In Demo 5, the "Heat" mechanic introduced during the interrogation sequence adds a layer of complexity. If your character stays "heated" for too long, you will take chip damage every turn.

1.  **Master the Perfect Block:** This is the only way to avoid the "Heat" status effect entirely.
2.  **Manage your SP:** Don't blow all your Skill Points on the first wave of guards. Save them for the Interrogator’s second phase.
3.  **Use Items Strategically:** Pizza and other restorative items should be used when your HP drops below 40%, as the boss has a "Desperation Move" that deals high burst damage.

> **💡 Tip:** Equipping the "Ice Fragment" item before the interrogation begins can negate the first three stacks of Heat damage you receive during the fight.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I trigger the block tales demo 5 interrogation sequence?**

A: The interrogation is a mandatory part of the main story in Demo 5. It triggers automatically after you are captured in the "Underground Infiltration" questline. Ensure you have completed all previous Demo 4 content to access this area.

**Q: Can I skip the interrogation dialogue?**

A: While you can fast-forward through the text, your choices still matter. Skipping without looking at the prompts may lead you to accidentally select the "Defiant" path, which makes the ensuing fight much harder.

**Q: What is the best build for the Demo 5 boss solo?**

A: A "Defender-Utility" build is currently the most successful for solo players. Focus on cards that increase your block window and restore SP on defense, as you will be spending a lot of time protecting yourself from the boss's rapid-fire attacks.

**Q: Is there a way to remove the "Heat" debuff during the fight?**

A: Yes, using any "Ice" category item or the "Cooling Mist" skill will immediately clear the Heat status. Additionally, landing three Perfect Blocks in a row will naturally cool your character down.
