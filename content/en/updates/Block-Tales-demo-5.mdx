## Getting Started: The Journey to Trinity

The adventure in **Block Tales demo 5** begins at the HQ basement where you reunite with your team. Your primary objective is to reach Trinity, the largest city in the realm, to prevent the Corblocks from seizing the Windforce—the kingdom's most sacred relic. Following the death of King Cleos, the city is in a state of flux as it prepares for the coronation of Princess Cassie.

To get there, you must traverse "The Heights," a classic platforming area filled with Corblocks soldiers and pterodactyls. Along the way, you will encounter silver statues commemorating legendary duelists, which serve as landmarks for your progress.

## Mastering the Paintball Gun and Puzzles

One of the most significant additions in **Block Tales demo 5** is the Paintball Gun. Granted by the eccentric wizard Opticus the Magnificent after a survival challenge, this tool is essential for environmental puzzles. You will first use it under the guidance of Tutorial Terry to replicate drawings on canvases to open gates.

### How to use the Paintball Gun
To use the Paintball Gun, switch to it using the 'Q' key. You must stand on specific tiles and splatter the canvas to match the patterns provided in the environment. These puzzles become increasingly complex as you move through the Red and Blue Districts.

| Puzzle Type | Required Tool | Solution Hint |
|---|---|---|
| **Canvas Replication** | Paintball Gun | Mimic the board drawing exactly. |
| **Orb Activation** | Basic Attack | Strike the orb three times in quick succession. |
| **Bird & Stones** | Dynamite | Use explosive force on the bird-shaped mechanism. |
| **Ghost Path** | Ghost Potion | Use the potion to phase through specific obstacles. |

## Navigating the Trinity Districts

Once you enter Trinity, you must navigate three distinct districts to gain the support of the Trinity Council. Each district is guarded by a leader who requires proof of your intentions.

### The Red District (Elite Commander Reginald)
Reginald is a "hater" of outsiders and will challenge your presence immediately. After a series of skirmishes, you must face him in battle. He utilizes an **Axe Spin** attack that can deal significant multi-hit damage. Defeating him rewards you with a Sanctum Shard, though he remains skeptical of your heroics.

### The Blue District (Councilman Robert)
This area focuses on the legal and political side of Trinity. You will be put on trial and interrogated. Your answers here matter for your reputation, though the High Councilman ultimately seeks to see if you are a "good person."

### The Astral Isles (High Scholar Zuri)
The Astral Isles are a visually stunning area featuring cloud cars and floating platforms. Zuri tests your wisdom rather than your strength. You must use the **Minimize Ball** move to shrink and navigate small openings, and solve a telescope-based puzzle to "Seek the Source."

| **Trinity Council** | Various | RNG Magic Spells | Access to Sanctum |

## The Final Confrontation: The Seventh Sanctum

The climax of **Block Tales demo 5** takes place in the Seventh Sanctum. After the Trinity Council turns on you due to Reginald's influence, you must fight a 3-on-1 battle against the Council members. This is widely considered the hardest fight in the demo due to the RNG nature of the wizard's spells.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The wizard in the Council fight can cast a lightning bolt that inflicts "Dizzy." This status effect may cause you to hit yourself or your allies, potentially ending a successful run.

After defeating the Council, you enter the heart of the Sanctum to find Princess Cassie struggling with the "visions" of the Windforce. The Corblocks leader, Argel, appears with a massive frost dragon.

### Argel Boss Strategy
1.  **Protect Cassie:** She is a vital ally in this fight. Keep her HP high using items like Cheeseburgers.
2.  **Break the Armor:** Use Dynamite or heavy sword hits to chip away at the dragon's defenses.
3.  **Cassie’s Stabs:** Occasionally, Cassie will find an opening to stab the dragon for 10 damage. This is your cue to follow up with your strongest attacks.
4.  **Ice Dagger:** Use the Ice Dagger to inflict freeze or slow effects, giving you more time to heal.

## Rewards and Post-Game Content

Completing the demo grants you the **Windforce**, a legendary blade that increases your overworld utility by allowing you to destroy metal bars. Additionally, you unlock the **Cassie Summon Card**, a powerful special move that costs 3 Energy.

**Windforce Special Ability:**
*   Deals 5 Base Damage.
*   Inflicts "Slow" on all enemies for two turns.
*   Applies Level 3 Defense Down for five turns.

This sword is essential for players looking to 100% the current build, as previously inaccessible areas in older demos can now be opened using the Windforce's power. ## FAQ

**Q: How do I get the Paintball Gun in Block Tales demo 5?**

A: You must reach the Wizard Tower in The Heights and complete the survival challenge against Opticus the Magnificent. He will grant you the gun as a reward for proving your skill.

**Q: What are the correct answers for the Trinity Court interrogation?**

A: While there is some flexibility, generally choosing answers that emphasize self-defense and the protection of others (like "I saved a woman's life") will lead to a favorable verdict from High Councilman Robert.

**Q: How do I solve the Astral Isles telescope puzzle?**

A: You must interact with the telescope and follow the "truths" provided. This involves hitting the magical orb three times, using dynamite on the bird statue, and using a Ghost Potion to reach the final seal.

**Q: Can I use the Windforce in previous levels?**

A: Yes! Once you unlock the Windforce at the end of **Block Tales demo 5**, you can backtrack to earlier areas to break metal bars and discover secret chests or shortcuts you couldn't access before.
