## What is the First Aid Skill?

Before diving into the acquisition process, it is important to understand what makes First Aid a staple in almost every player's deck. Unlike potions, which are consumed upon use, First Aid is a Skill Card. Once equipped, it allows a character to spend a set amount of SP to restore HP to themselves or an ally during their turn.

In the current 2026 version of the game, First Aid scales slightly with your stats, but its primary value lies in its reliability. It allows your "healer" or support-focused character to stay active in long dungeon crawls where inventory space is at a premium.

| Attribute | Value | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Skill Type** | Support / Healing | Restores HP to a single target. |
| **SP Cost** | 3 SP | Relatively low cost for early to mid-game. |
| **Target** | Single Ally | Can be used on self or a teammate. |
| **Card Slots** | 1 Slot | Minimal impact on your total card capacity. |

> **💡 Tip:** Always pair First Aid with SP-restoring items or cards like "Happy Flower" to ensure you never run out of healing potential during boss fights.

## Block Tales How to Get First Aid: The Quest Walkthrough

To unlock this ability, you must complete a specific side quest often referred to as the "Quest for the First Aid Kit." This quest becomes available relatively early in the game, specifically once you have access to the main hub area following the introductory sequence.

### Step 1: Locating the Doctor
The quest begins in the central town. You need to find the NPC known as the Doctor (or the Medic in some localizations). He is typically found near the clinic or the central plaza. When you speak to him, he will mention that he has lost his medical supplies or that he requires assistance in retrieving a specific kit that was stolen or misplaced during a monster raid.

### Step 2: Navigating to the Quest Area
Once you accept the quest, your map will likely update with a marker pointing toward the outskirts of the town or a nearby forest area. You will need to prepare for combat, as the path to the First Aid Kit is guarded by standard mobs. 

### Step 3: Retrieving the Kit
In the designated area, you will find a treasure chest or a dropped item interactable that contains the "Lost Medical Supplies." In some versions of the quest, you may need to defeat a mini-boss or a wave of enemies before the item becomes accessible. 

### Step 4: Returning for the Reward
Head back to the Doctor in the hub town. Upon handing over the supplies, he will reward you with the First Aid Skill Card. This is the definitive answer for **Block Tales how to get first aid**—it is a guaranteed reward for helping the town's medical professional.

## SP Management for Healers

Acquiring the skill is only half the battle; using it effectively is where the strategy lies. Since First Aid consumes 3 SP, you cannot spam it indefinitely without a plan. In Block Tales, SP is a shared resource or a limited individual pool depending on your current build.

To maximize your healing, consider the following:
1. **Defensive Buffs:** Use "Guard" to reduce incoming damage. If you take less damage, you need to heal less often, saving SP for offensive skills.
2. **SP Regeneration:** Equip cards that grant SP back upon successful timed hits or at the start of a turn.
3. **Item Synergy:** Keep a few "Honey" or "Juice" items in your inventory. These restore SP, essentially acting as "fuel" for your First Aid skill.

## Comparing First Aid to Consumable Items

Is it always better to use First Aid? Not necessarily. In high-intensity battles where you need to heal and attack in the same turn, using an item might be faster if your character has specific "Fast Item" perks. However, for general progression, the skill card is superior.

| Healing Method | Cost | Efficiency | Best Use Case |
|---|---|---|---|
| **First Aid Skill** | **3 SP** | High (Renewable) | Long dungeons and sustained fights. |
| **Small Potion** | **10 Coins** | Medium (Limited) | Emergency healing when SP is empty. |
| **Cherry/Food** | **5 Coins** | Low | Out-of-combat top-offs. |
| **Life Shard** | **Rare Drop** | Very High | Reviving fallen allies (First Aid cannot revive). |

> **⚠️ Warning:** First Aid cannot be used if a character is at 0 HP. You must use a Life Shard or a similar revival item first before First Aid can be applied to restore their health to safe levels.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I get the First Aid skill card without doing the quest?**

A: Currently, in 2026, the quest is the primary way to obtain the card. While some high-level enemies may rarely drop it, the quest provided by the Doctor is the only guaranteed method for early-game players.

**Q: Does First Aid scale with my character's level?**

A: Yes, the amount of healing provided by First Aid increases slightly as your base stats grow. Additionally, certain "Healer" badges can boost the effectiveness of all healing skills, including First Aid.

**Q: What is the best way to manage SP when using First Aid frequently?**

A: To keep your SP pool healthy while using **Block Tales how to get first aid**, focus on landing "Perfect" timed hits and blocks. Successful timing often rewards players with small amounts of SP, which adds up over a long encounter.

**Q: Is First Aid better than the "Group Heal" skill?**

A: Group Heal is better for recovery after a major AOE attack, but it costs significantly more SP (usually 8-10). First Aid is much more efficient for targeted healing throughout a standard battle.
