## Step-by-Step: How to Beat the Trinity Council in Block Tales

The fight unfolds in distinct stages. Managing your resources across these stages is the difference between a victory and a "Game Over" screen.

### Phase 1: The Initial Onslaught
In the first few turns, all three members will attack. Your primary goal here is survival. Do not worry about dealing massive damage immediately. Instead, focus on learning the block timings for the Enforcer's hammer swing and the Mystic's energy bolts. 

1. **Apply Buffs:** Use your first turn to set up any defensive cards or stat-boosting items.
2. **Target the Tactician:** Use **Power Bounce** or high-damage single-target skills to chip away at the Tactician.
3. **Perfect Blocks:** You must hit your "Action Commands" for defense. A failed block can result in losing 25% of your health in a single turn.

### Phase 2: Managing the Mid-Game
Once one member falls (ideally the Tactician), the remaining two will enter an "Enraged" state. Their attack patterns will become faster and include more hits. This is where the **Defender** card becomes your best friend. Even if you miss a perfect block, the flat defense increase will prevent you from being knocked out.

### Phase 3: The Final Stand
When only one member remains, they will often use a "Desperation Move." This is a cinematic, high-damage attack that requires multiple timed hits to fully block. Keep your SP high during the end of Phase 2 so you can spam your strongest finishers to end the fight before they can cycle through their ultimate move multiple times.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Never let your HP drop below 6. The Council's "Triple Strike" combo can deal exactly 5 damage even through a standard block.

## FAQ

**Q: What level should I be to beat the Trinity Council?**

A: It is highly recommended to be at least Level 8 or 9. While it is possible at lower levels, the HP pool provided by these levels gives you the necessary cushion to survive missed blocks.

**Q: Which council member should I kill first?**

A: Most players find success by targeting the Tactician first. Their ability to buff the other members and coordinate multi-hit attacks makes them the most dangerous threat in the long run.

**Q: Can I beat the Trinity Council solo?**

A: Yes, the fight scales based on the number of players. When playing solo, the Council has lower total HP, but you must be much more precise with your blocks since all attacks will be directed at you.

**Q: What happens if I lose the fight?**

A: You will be sent back to the last save point (usually the Bed or Statue before the boss room). You do not lose your items permanently if you reload, so feel free to experiment with different card combinations to see **how to beat the trinity council in block tales** using your specific playstyle.
