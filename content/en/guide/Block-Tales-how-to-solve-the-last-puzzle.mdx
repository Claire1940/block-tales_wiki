## Step 2: Calibrating the Color Wheel

After the symbols are set, your attention must turn to the color wheel mechanism. This device controls the elemental flow of the room and dictates which type of orb will be accepted by the final pedestal. 

In Demo 4, the solution requires the color wheel to be set to **Red**. Rotating the wheel past Blue, Green, and Yellow is necessary until the crimson light illuminates the center of the chamber. This color choice is not arbitrary; it corresponds to the heat or "fire" element required to power the ancient machinery found in the Depths.

1.  Interact with the wheel's handle or pressure plate.
2.  Observe the light reflected on the nearby walls.
3.  Stop rotating immediately when the room takes on a reddish hue.

## Step 3: Positioning the Pedestal Block

The next step in the **Block Tales how to solve the last puzzle** process is moving the physical block. > **⚠️ Warning:** Be careful not to push the block into a corner where it might get stuck. If the block becomes unresponsive, you may need to exit the room and re-enter to reset its position.

## Step 4: The Red Orb Placement

The final piece of the puzzle is the Red Orb itself. This item is usually located on a high shelf or held by a statue within the same chamber. Once you have set the symbols to match and turned the color wheel to Red, the protective barrier around the orb should dissipate.
