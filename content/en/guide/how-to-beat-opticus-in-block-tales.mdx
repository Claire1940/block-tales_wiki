## How to Beat Opticus in Block Tales: Move Set Analysis

Understanding the boss's animations is the most critical part of the encounter. Opticus uses a variety of ocular-themed attacks that vary in timing. Missing a block on his heavy-hitting "Gaze" attacks can result in losing nearly half your health in a single turn.

### Primary Attack Patterns

| Attack Name | Description | How to Counter |
|---|---|---|
| **Focused Beam** | A fast, single-target laser shot. | Press the block button exactly when the eye flashes red. |
| **Ocular Barrage** | Multiple small projectiles fired in sequence. | Requires rhythmic tapping; watch the projectile flight path. |
| **Void Gaze** | A screen-wide explosion dealing massive damage. | The hardest to time; wait for the eye to shrink before the blast. |
| **Summon Minions** | Opticus calls smaller eyes to assist him. | Use AoE (Area of Effect) skills to clear them immediately. |

The "Void Gaze" is particularly dangerous because it often inflicts status ailments. Keeping your Defense stat boosted via badges or items can mitigate the risk of a "Game Over" if your timing is slightly off.

## Step-by-Step Battle Strategy

When the fight begins, do not immediately use your most powerful attacks. Opticus often has a high defense value in his initial state. Instead, spend the first few turns buffing your character and observing his patterns.

### Phase 1: The Observation
In the first phase, Opticus will mostly use **Focused Beam**. Use this time to get comfortable with the block timing. If you have "Power Soda," use it on turn two. Aim to keep your HP above 80% at all times.

### Phase 2: The Summoning
Once Opticus reaches 60% HP, he will begin summoning minions. This is the most dangerous part of the fight because the minions can distract you from Opticus's own animations. Use an AoE skill like "Earthquake" or "Fire Rain" to clear the field. If the minions stay on the board for more than two turns, they will begin to heal the boss.

### Phase 3: The Desperation Phase
At 25% HP, Opticus's eye will turn a dark shade of purple. He will now use **Void Gaze** every other turn. During this phase, focus entirely on survival. Only attack when you are at full health. If you are low on SP, use your "Star Candy" now.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not attempt to "tank" the Void Gaze without a defense buff. Even with a successful block, the chip damage can be lethal if your base defense is low.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

Many players fail when trying to figure out **how to beat opticus in block tales** because they ignore the secondary mechanics of the Pit. Remember that you have fought 99 floors of enemies before this; your resources are likely depleted.

*   **Poor Badge Management**: Entering the fight with "Money Magnet" or other utility badges instead of combat-focused ones.
